About Jarumi Ventures Africa

Building resilient, revenue-generating, and impact-driven institutions across Africa.

Who We Are

Jarumi Ventures Africa is a strategic advisory and systems-strengthening firm supporting nonprofits and social impact organizations to transition from donor dependency to sustainability.

Our Mission

To empower purpose-driven organizations with systems and strategies that scale impact sustainably.

Our Vision

A thriving African social sector built on resilience, accountability, and sustainability.

Our Story

Jarumi Ventures Africa was founded to bridge the gap between impact and sustainability by embedding strategic, operational, and revenue-focused systems.
